Our Legacy

Where History Meets Hospitality

Nestled in the sacred city of Kushinagar, Hotel Legacy stands as a testament to the rich Buddhist heritage of the region. Our property has been welcoming pilgrims and travelers for over three decades, offering a unique blend of traditional warmth and modern comforts.

Kushinagar, where Lord Buddha attained Mahaparinirvana, is one of the most important pilgrimage sites for Buddhists worldwide. At Hotel Legacy, we strive to create an environment that reflects the peace and spirituality of this holy land.

Our architecture draws inspiration from ancient Buddhist monasteries, while our interiors showcase exquisite local craftsmanship. Every corner of our hotel tells a story of the region's glorious past and its continuing spiritual significance.
